UEFA Champions League

This annual football event involves the biggest football clubs in Europe clashing against each other.
It's always great to see the big clubs contest for the most desired title in Europe!
Another amazing thing is the incredible anthem played before every game. I hope that they never change it!

The FIFA World Cup™ brings together countries from different continents. Home of big shocks, surprises, and entertaining matches,
this quadrennial event has every footballing nation figthing to be acquire the supreme title as world champions, adding a golden star to their team's jersey.
